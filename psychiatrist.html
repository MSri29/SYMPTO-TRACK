Symptoms Disorder Recommended Treatment
Persistent sadness, loss of interest Depression Therapy, Antidepressants (SSRIs)
Excessive worry, restlessness Anxiety Disorder Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Anti-anxiety medications
Mood swings, extreme highs & lows Bipolar Disorder Mood stabilizers, Therapy
Hallucinations, disorganized thoughts Schizophrenia Antipsychotic medications, Supportive therapy
Feeling sad or down, low energy, loss of interest Depressive disorders (Major Depressive Disorder, etc.) Antidepressants, psychotherapy (CBT, interpersonal therapy), lifestyle changes.
Excessive fears or worries, feeling of dread, panic attacks Anxiety disorders (Generalized Anxiety Disorder, Panic Disorder, Phobias) Anti-anxiety medications, antidepressants, psychotherapy (CBT, exposure therapy), relaxation techniques.
Extreme mood changes (highs and lows), mania, depression Bipolar and related disorders Mood stabilizers, antipsychotic medications, psychotherapy, lifestyle management.
Detachment from reality, delusions, hallucinations, disorganized thinking Schizophrenia spectrum and other psychotic disorders Antipsychotic medications, psychotherapy, social support.
Preoccupations or obsessions, repetitive thoughts and actions Obsessive-compulsive and related disorders (OCD, Hoarding Disorder, etc.) Antidepressants, psychotherapy (CBT, exposure and response prevention).
Trouble coping after stressful events, flashbacks, nightmares, hypervigilance Trauma- and stressor-related disorders (PTSD, Acute Stress Disorder) Psychotherapy (CBT, EMDR), antidepressants, anti-anxiety medications.
Disrupted sense of self, memory loss, multiple identities Dissociative disorders (Dissociative Identity Disorder, Dissociative Amnesia) Psychotherapy (trauma-focused therapy), addressing underlying trauma.
Physical symptoms with emotional distress, illness anxiety Somatic symptom and related disorders Psychotherapy (CBT), addressing underlying emotional distress, managing symptoms.
Disturbances related to eating, extreme weight loss or gain, distorted body image Feeding and eating disorders (Anorexia Nervosa, Binge-Eating Disorder) Psychotherapy (CBT, family-based therapy), nutritional counseling, medical monitoring.
Inappropriate elimination of urine or stool Elimination disorders (Enuresis) Behavioral therapy, medication (in some cases).
Problems with sleep, insomnia, sleep apnea Sleep-wake disorders (Insomnia, Sleep Apnea) Behavioral therapy, medication, lifestyle changes, addressing underlying medical conditions.
Problems with self-control, impulsive behaviors, aggression Disruptive, impulse-control, and conduct disorders Psychotherapy (CBT, anger management), medication (if co-occurring conditions exist).
Excessive use of substances, addictive behaviors Substance-related and addictive disorders Psychotherapy, medication-assisted treatment, support groups, rehabilitation.
Decline in cognitive function, memory loss, confusion Neurocognitive disorders (Alzheimer's, TBI-related) Medications (to manage symptoms), supportive care, therapy.
Lasting patterns of emotional instability, unhealthy behaviors Personality disorders (Borderline, Antisocial, Narcissistic) Psychotherapy (DBT, schema therapy), medication (for co-occurring conditions).
Sexual interests causing distress or harm Paraphilic disorders Psychotherapy, medication, legal intervention if required.
Wide range of behavioral or emotional problems in childhood Neurodevelopmental disorders (ADHD, Autism Spectrum Disorder, Learning Disorders) Behavioral therapy, occupational therapy, medication (in some cases).
Unexplained aches and pains, changes in eating habits, suicidal thinking, withdrawal from activities, confused thinking. General Mental Illness Symptoms Psychotherapy, Medication, lifestyle changes, support groups.
Distorted body image, preoccupation with perceived flaws Body Dysmorphic Disorder (BDD) CBT, antidepressants (SSRIs).
Intense emotional instability, impulsive behavior, unstable relationships Borderline Personality Disorder (BPD) Dialectical behavior therapy (DBT), schema therapy, medication (for co-occurring conditions).
Persistent difficulty discarding possessions, severe distress related to saving items Hoarding Disorder CBT, exposure and response prevention.
Recurrent pulling out of hair, resulting in hair loss Trichotillomania CBT, habit reversal therapy, medication (in some cases).
Recurrent skin picking, leading to skin lesions Excoriation (Skin-Picking) Disorder CBT, habit reversal therapy, medication (in some cases).
Significant distress or impairment related to gender identity Gender Dysphoria Psychotherapy, hormone therapy, surgery (if desired)
Recurrent episodes of binge eating followed by compensatory behaviors (e.g., purging) Bulimia Nervosa CBT, nutritional counseling, medical monitoring, antidepressants.
Recurrent episodes of binge eating without compensatory behaviors Binge Eating Disorder CBT, interpersonal psychotherapy, nutritional counseling, medication (in some cases).
Persistent patterns of negativistic, hostile, and defiant behavior Oppositional Defiant Disorder (ODD) Parent training, family therapy, CBT.
Repetitive, involuntary movements and vocalizations (tics) Tourette Syndrome Behavioral therapies (e.g., CBIT), medication (to manage tics).
