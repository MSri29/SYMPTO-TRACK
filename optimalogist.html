Redness, severe pain, foreign body sensation, tearing, discharge, blurred vision, light sensitivity, white spot on cornea, swollen eyelids Corneal Ulcer (Keratitis) Antibiotic, antifungal, or antiviral eye drops, pain relievers.

Pain, redness, rash, blurred vision, tearing, discharge, light sensitivity Herpes Keratitis Antiviral medications (topical or oral).

Blurred/double vision, bruising, swelling, numbness, blood in eye, difficulty moving eye, flattened cheek, bulging/sunken eyeballs, intense cheek pain Orbital Fracture Surgery (in severe cases), pain relievers, observation.
Red blood spots on the white of the eye Subconjunctival Hemorrhage Artificial tears (if needed), usually heals on its own.
Red, swollen, itchy eyes, burning/tearing, light sensitivity Eye Allergies (Allergic Conjunctivitis) Artificial tears, decongestants, antihistamine/mast-cell stabilizers, steroid eye drops.
Cloudy vision, glare, faded colors, difficulty with night vision Cataracts Surgery to remove the cloudy lens and replace it with an artificial lens.
Gradual peripheral vision loss, eye pain, headaches, blurred vision Glaucoma Eye drops, laser treatment, or surgery to lower eye pressure
Blurred central vision, distorted vision, difficulty recognizing faces Age-related Macular Degeneration (AMD) Anti-VEGF injections, laser therapy, vitamin supplements.
Fluctuating blurred vision, double vision, eye pain, redness, light sensitivity Uveitis Corticosteroid eye drops, oral corticosteroids, immunosuppressants.
Dry, gritty, burning eyes, blurred vision, light sensitivity Dry Eye Syndrome Artificial tears, lubricating eye ointments, prescription eye drops, punctal plugs.
Distorted central vision, blurry vision, dark or blind spots Macular Degeneration (AMD) Anti-VEGF injections, laser therapy, vitamin supplements.
Blurred vision, double vision, eye pain, vision loss Optic Neuritis Corticosteroids, treatment of underlying conditions (e.g., multiple sclerosis).
Eye pain, headache, nausea, vomiting, blurred vision, halos Acute Angle-Closure Glaucoma Emergency medical treatment to rapidly lower eye pressure, laser iridotomy, medications.
Distorted vision, especially straight lines appearing wavy, blurry vision Macular Edema Treating the underlying cause (e.g., diabetes), anti-VEGF injections, corticosteroids.
Double vision, eye misalignment, loss of depth perception Strabismus (crossed eyes) Eye patches, eyeglasses, eye exercises, surgery.
Reduced vision in one eye, often detected in childhood Amblyopia (lazy eye) Eye patches, eyeglasses, vision therapy
