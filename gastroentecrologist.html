Peptic Ulcers Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs): Lansoprazole, Omeprazole (help heal the ulcer), Antibiotics: Amoxicillin, Clarithromycin, Metronidazole (treat H. pylori infection), Antacids: Magnesium hydroxide, Calcium carbonate (reduce acid and soothe pain), H2 blockers: Ranitidine, Famotidine (reduce stomach acid)
Unexplained weight loss, stomach pain, nausea, vomiting, loss of appetite, indigestion. Stomach Cancer (Gastric Cancer) Chemotherapy: Fluorouracil, Cisplatin (to treat cancer), Targeted therapies: Trastuzumab (if HER2 positive), Pain relievers: Acetaminophen, Opioids (for pain relief), Radiation therapy: Used to shrink tumors or relieve symptoms

Abdominal pain, bloating, diarrhea, constipation, changes in bowel habits. Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS) Antispasmodics: Dicyclomine, Hyoscine (for abdominal pain and cramping), Antidiarrheals: Loperamide (for diarrhea), Laxatives: Polyethylene glycol (for constipation), Fiber supplements: Psyllium (to help regulate bowel movements), Probiotics: Lactobacillus (help balance gut bacteria)

Gastroenteritis Antiemetics: Ondansetron, Promethazine (for nausea and vomiting), Antidiarrheals: Loperamide (for diarrhea), Rehydration solutions: Oral rehydration salts (to prevent dehydration), Antibiotics (if bacterial infection is present): Ciprofloxacin, Azithromycin
Heartburn, chest pain, regurgitation, difficulty swallowing. Hiatal Hernia Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs): Omeprazole, Esomeprazole (to control acid reflux), Antacids: Tums, Mylanta (to neutralize stomach acid), H2 blockers: Ranitidine, Famotidine (to reduce acid production)
Diarrhea, abdominal pain, bloating, weight loss, fatigue. Celiac Disease Gluten-free diet: Strict avoidance of gluten (primary treatment), Corticosteroids: Prednisone (for inflammation), Enzyme supplements: Digestive enzymes (to help with digestion)
